Maintenance re-release of 1.2.0. The 1.2.0 release build was not published because of a
continuous-integration misconfiguration; 1.2.1 fixes the release pipeline and ships the same
app. No changes to how the app looks or behaves since 1.2.0.

(1.2.0 brought: the correct profile loading at service start instead of a brief Battery Saver
flash; PWM-sensitive brightness read-out tracking perceived brightness; circadian falling back
to a cached sun position with staleness hints; a redesigned Profiles & Contexts screen with a
pinned Load / Save / Contexts bar, active-profile highlighting, and confirmation prompts before
deleting or overwriting.)
